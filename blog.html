Ottawa - The capital of Canada, Ottawa is located in southeast Ontario, near the U.S. border and neighboring city of Montreal. During the summer, the park-lined Rideau Canal is overflowing with boats; during the winter however, visitors will find plenty of ice-skaters taking advantage of the frozen conditions.
